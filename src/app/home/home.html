Rare Disease Registry

Welcome to the RaDaR National Registry of Rare Kidney Diseases. In partnership with Rare Disease Groups, RaDaR facilitates translational and epidemiological research into rare diseases through a comprehensive clinical database. RaDaR is managed by the UK Kidney Association. For more information about RaDaR please go to rarerenal.org.
